Exercise & Wellness

Knee to Chest Stretch

Pull knees toward chest and hold.

1. Lie flat on your back. 2. Bend both knees and bring them toward your chest. 3. Wrap your arms around your knees. 4. Hold for 30–60 seconds while breathing deeply.

Reduces bloating

Wind Relieving Pose

Releases trapped gas.

1. Lie on your back. 2. Bring one knee to chest. 3. Hold with both hands. 4. Switch legs and repeat.

Improves digestion & reduces bloating

Seated Forward Bend

Gentle abdominal compression.

1. Sit with legs stretched. 2. Bend forward slowly. 3. Try touching toes. 4. Hold for 20–30 seconds.

Reduces bloating

Pelvic Tilt

Lift pelvis slowly upward.

1. Lie on your back with knees bent. 2. Keep feet flat on the floor. 3. Tighten your stomach muscles. 4. Gently lift your pelvis upward. 5. Hold for 5 seconds and relax.

Relieves cramps

Supine Twist

Twisting lower body gently.

1. Lie on your back. 2. Bend one knee. 3. Twist it across body. 4. Hold 20 seconds and switch sides.

Relaxes lower back & eases cramps

Bridge Pose

Strengthens pelvic area.

1. Lie on back. 2. Bend knees. 3. Lift hips upward. 4. Hold 10 seconds and relax.

Improves blood flow & reduces pain

Happy Baby Pose

Opens hip muscles.

1. Lie on back. 2. Hold feet with hands. 3. Pull knees toward armpits. 4. Rock gently side to side.

Eases menstrual discomfort

Neck Stretch

Stretch neck gently side to side.

1. Sit or stand straight. 2. Slowly tilt your head toward the right shoulder. 3. Hold for 10 seconds. 4. Repeat on the left side.

Reduces stiffness

Shoulder Rolls

Relieves upper body tension.

1. Sit or stand straight. 2. Roll shoulders forward 10 times. 3. Roll backward 10 times.

Improves posture

Standing Side Stretch

Stretches side body.

1. Stand straight. 2. Raise one arm. 3. Bend toward opposite side. 4. Hold 15 seconds and switch.

Reduces body stiffness

Hamstring Stretch

Improves leg flexibility.

1. Sit with legs extended. 2. Bend forward slowly. 3. Hold 20 seconds.

Reduces leg tightness

Child Pose

Relax forward sitting on heels.

1. Kneel on the floor. 2. Sit back on your heels. 3. Stretch your arms forward. 4. Lower your chest toward thighs. 5. Hold for 1–2 minutes while breathing slowly.

Calms mind

Butterfly Pose

Sit and flap knees slowly.

1. Sit upright on the floor. 2. Bring soles of your feet together. 3. Hold your feet with both hands. 4. Gently move knees up and down slowly.

Improves pelvic circulation

Legs Up The Wall

Improves circulation.

1. Lie near a wall. 2. Raise legs up against wall. 3. Relax arms beside body. 4. Stay 2–5 minutes.

Reduces stress & fatigue

Deep Breathing

Pranayama relaxation.

1. Sit comfortably. 2. Inhale deeply for 4 seconds. 3. Hold for 4 seconds. 4. Exhale slowly for 6 seconds.

Reduces anxiety & balances mood

Corpse Pose

Complete relaxation pose.

1. Lie flat on your back. 2. Keep arms relaxed. 3. Close eyes. 4. Breathe naturally for 5 minutes.

Improves sleep & mental calmness

Cobra Stretch

Lift chest upward while lying.

1. Lie on your stomach. 2. Place palms under shoulders. 3. Slowly lift your chest upward. 4. Keep hips touching the floor. 5. Hold for 15–20 seconds.

Relieves abdominal pain
